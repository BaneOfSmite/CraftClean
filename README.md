**Description:**

Surround a piece of coal with 8 smeltable items in a crafting bench to get 8 out of the smelted result.

---

**MC Version:**

1.8.X Only

---

**Commands:**

    /craftclean enable/disable
- Toggles craftclean enable/disable

---

**Permissions:**

    Bane.Op
- Enables the usage of /craftclean
